Ingrediants:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup flower
  • 1/3 teaspoon baking powder

optional ingrediants:

  • frosting

steps to make:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in pan, and then stir sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Then the cocoa, flour, and baking powder.
  3. Spread butter into pan
  4. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
  5. If you want frosting, frost while still warm.
